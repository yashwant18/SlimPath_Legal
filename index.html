Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Your privacy matters to us. SlimPath is a personal health tool. We collect only what is necessary to make the app work and we never sell your data to anyone.

1. Who we are

SlimPath is an Android application developed independently. References to "we", "us", or "our" refer to the SlimPath developer. You can contact us at any time at the email address listed at the bottom of this page.

2. What data we collect

We collect only the information you directly provide to us when using the app:

  • Google account information — your name and email address, collected when you sign in with Google. Used solely to identify your account and sync your data across devices.
  • Body metrics — your current weight, target weight, height, and gender. Used to calculate your personalised daily goals and track your progress.
  • Goal completion data — which daily tasks you mark as done or missed, per day.
  • Weight log entries — weight readings you manually enter over time.
  • Notes — personal notes you add to individual daily goals.
  • App settings — your notification preferences and custom goal targets.

We do not collect:

  • Location data
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Device identifiers beyond what Firebase Auth requires
  • Browsing history or behaviour outside the app
  • Payment or financial information

3. How we use your data

  • To calculate your BMR, TDEE, calorie target, step goal, and other personalised daily goals
  • To track your streak, missed days, and progress over time
  • To sync your data across devices when you log in with the same Google account
  • To send you daily reminder notifications (only if you enable this in Settings)
  • To show you estimated weight progress based on your journey data

We use your data exclusively to operate the SlimPath app for your benefit. We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

4. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database service operated by Google LLC. Firebase stores data on secure servers and encrypts data in transit and at rest.

Data is stored in Google Cloud regions. By using SlimPath, you consent to your data being processed by Google Firebase in accordance with Firebase's Privacy Policy.

A local copy of your data is also kept on your device using Android's SharedPreferences storage, so the app works offline.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties, except:

  • Google Firebase — as the infrastructure provider for authentication and database storage
  • Google Sign-In — to authenticate your identity securely
  • Google AdMob — if you are a free-tier user, anonymised advertising identifiers may be used to show relevant ads. Premium users see no ads and no advertising data is collected for them.

We do not share your health data, weight, or goal completion data with any third party under any circumstances.

6. Advertising (free tier only)

Free-tier users see ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device advertising identifiers to show relevant ads. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalised ads by going to your device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

Premium subscribers see zero ads. No advertising SDKs are active for premium users.

7. Your rights

You have the following rights over your data at any time:

  • Access — view all your stored data directly within the app
  • Correction — update your weight, goals, and profile in the app
  • Deletion — delete all your data by going to Settings → Delete account & all data. This permanently and immediately removes all your data from our servers.
  • Export — contact us to request a copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent — sign out and delete your account at any time

8. Data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • Open SlimPath → Settings tab
  • Scroll to Danger Zone
  • Tap "Delete account & all data"

This action permanently removes all your data from Firebase within seconds. It cannot be undone. You will be signed out immediately.

Alternatively, email us at privacy@slimpath.app and we will delete your data within 7 days.

9. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all data is removed immediately. We do not retain backup copies of deleted account data.

10. Children's privacy

SlimPath is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

11. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data including:

  • All data transmitted between the app and Firebase is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase Firestore security rules ensure users can only access their own data
  • Google Sign-In is used for authentication — we never store passwords
  • Your body metrics are never transmitted to any server other than Firebase

12. Third-party services

SlimPath uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database
  • Google Sign-In — account authentication
  • Google AdMob — advertising (free tier only)

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app. Continued use of SlimPath after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact us

For any privacy questions, data requests, or concerns, contact us at:

📧 privacy@slimpath.app
We respond to all privacy requests within 7 business days.
